Service Purchasing




01.Browse Services

  • Visit the Webbuilders website and navigate to the services section.
  • Browse available services including software development, hosting plans, and domain registration.

02. Select Services:

  • Select the desired service(s) by clicking the relevant option or “Add to Cart” button.
  • Customize your selection, such as choosing a hosting plan or providing software development requirements.

03.Review Cart:

  • Access your shopping cart to review selected services, quantities, and associated costs.
  • Make any necessary changes or additions before proceeding.

04.Account Creation:
  • Create a new account by providing your name, email address, and password if you are a new user.
  • Sign in using your existing credentials if you already have an account.

05.Billing and Payment:

  • Provide billing details including your name, address, and payment information.
  • Select a preferred payment method such as credit card or PayPal.
  • Review the total order amount including applicable taxes or fees.

06.Review and Confirm:

  • Review the order summary including selected services and costs.
  • Ensure all provided information is accurate and up to date.
  • Read and accept the terms & conditions, refund policy, and privacy policy.
  • Click the “Confirm Order” button to complete the purchase.

07.Order Confirmation:

  • Receive an order confirmation via email or on-screen notification.
  • Confirmation includes order number, purchased services, and billing details.

08.Service Provisioning:

  • Receive instructions to access hosting control panels or submit software development requirements.
  • For domain registration, receive DNS configuration and activation details.

09.Customer Support:

  • Contact Webbuilders customer support for assistance via email, phone, or live chat.
